About Us

IT STARTED, QUITE SIMPLY, LIKE THIS...

Food Funday Restaurant serves genuine Filipino cuisine with a real Filipino ambience. Located on Polo, Dapitan City, resident musicians play serenades during lunchtime, before being joined by dancers in the evening, offering guests a very pleasant show while they enjoy their dinner. If you come to Dapitan City and want to have an authentic Filipino experience, then Food Funday's should be your place of choice.
